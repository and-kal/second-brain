There's certain types of music, where when you go to an underground show, you want to make sure beforehand that you're not stepping into some pit of right-wingers. Black Metal being the obvious one. Noise and industrial as well, though much less so these days I'd say. I wouldn't have considered Dungeon Synth to fall into this category, too. Especially not that branch of it that is using [cutesy 8-bit aesthetics](https://sweet8bitterness.bandcamp.com/). But there I was, hearing about Moospirscher, Ulkurzkul & Mythenmetz playing a secret open air show on a ramshackle bridge at sundown (which promised to be quite a vibey experience - which it was) and having never heard of these acts before, checking them out online online to find some of them posing with the most obvious retro 90s German neonazi insignia on their IG: [jump boots and a bulldog](https://www.instagram.com/moospirscher/p/DAjaHcnCsbf/), and a [Böhse Onkels (sic!) shirt](https://www.instagram.com/teufelsmoor1/p/C8myQ1fCdTs). 

I was glad when the promoters reassured me that it's gonna be a nazi-free event and indeed the audience looked anything but (crypto-)right-wing or gray area. One of the live acts was even wearing a shirt of a German punk band; however, out of all Deutschpunk bands he chose one of the most right-leaning ones, namely Cotzbrocken. Now, Cotzbrocken are so dull and dumb that you can't really enjoy their music, so wearing this shirt is automatically more of a tongue-in-cheek gesture or joke. On the other hand, this tightrope walking and flirting with politically conservative positions coincides with the overall appearance of the live acts and with general problems I see in the dungeon synth genre.

[...]

Musically, I enjoyed the evening quite a bit. I missed Mythenmetz and came before Ulkurzkul played, who delivered some naïve and gloomy synth melodies on his Microkorg drowned in noise and distorted vocals. After putting on his polypore mask, Moospirscher did a similar live set, though it also included tape loops and samples, was richer in variety and quite captivating in the atmosphere it created. The ambiance there was nice, too, and I appreciate these folks who set up shows in a true DIY manner. Just putting a little pair of speakers on some random place and jar for tips and then spread it through word-of-mouth primarily. 

[...]
So I don't know who these guys are and their releases don't seem as dodgy as other labels' that some of their artists released on. Like this guy "Grimetat", who has a split tape with some artists called "Sonnenwendfeier" and "Volksseele" - out on Wotanic Pride. Diesen komischen Salat aus Begriffen aus dem Dritten Reich ist völlig zurückgeblieben. Bei den genannten Artists ist es wahrscheinlich sogar ernstgemeint, aber auch bei anderen Labels, wo diese (Neo-)Nazi-Symboliken vermutlich nur ein Spiel mit Zeichen sein soll (wenn man sich die Logos von [Menschenfeind Propaganda](https://menschenfeindpropaganda.bandcamp.com/) oder [Kraft & Wut](https://www.discogs.com/label/3672158-Kraft-Wut), wo Moospirscher ebenfalls veröffentlicht hat, anschaut), ist diese Ästhetik völlig abgedroschen. Kürzlich habe ich V. Klemperers "LTI" wiedergelesen und bei der Lektüre wurde mir klar, wie arm die (linguistische und Symbol-)Sprache der Nazis eigentlich war - und nach wie vor ist. Man hat es dort mit endlosen Variationen einiger weniger Grundbausteine zu tun: nordische Mythologie, biblisch aufgeladene Superlativierungen, ein paar begriffe aus dem altdeutschen Sprachschatz (oder irgendwelche Deutschtümelnden Neuschöpfungen). An dieser Spracharmut hat sich bis heute nichts geändert [...]

Grade, wenn man sich in einem Genre bewegt, dessen [Ursprünge](https://dungeonsynth.proboards.com/thread/1554/help-staying-away-dungeon-synth) oder [Ausläufer](https://www.reddit.com/r/rabm/comments/1cg9o2x/leftist_dungeon_synthkellersyntht%C3%A4nzelcore/) voller Nazis sind, finde ich es fahrlässig, immer weiter zu dogwhistlen und sich in Uneindeutigkeit zu hüllen, anstatt sich klar zu positionieren. Mir kommt dabei oft das Argument unter, dass diese Zwielichtigkeit grade den Reiz bei bestimmten untergrundigen Musikrichtungen ausmacht (man vergleiche: Powerelectronics-Projekte von dämlichen goons wie [Max Presch](https://www.vice.com/de/article/markus-presch-pmg-vs-frank-gossner-der-labelbetreiber-der-frueher-goebbels-zitierte-afrofunk/) oder eine Black-Metal-Act deiner Wahl).
